    I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Lehigh University. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Rutgers University. My research interests broadly encompass the areas of Cyber-Physical Systems, Human-Centered Computing, and Data Science. Currently, my work focuses on urban sensing, prediction, and decision-making, with diverse applications in Intelligent Transportation Systems, Logistics, On-demand Gig Economy, Last-mile Mobility, and Smart Cities.

    I always look for motivated graduate/undergraduate/visiting students to work with me. Please email me your CV and all the transcripts if you are interested.
