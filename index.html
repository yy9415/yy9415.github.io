About me

I am a PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science at Rutgers University. Before coming to Rutgers, I obtained my Bachelor degree from Northeastern University in China. My research interests broadly lie in mobile, sensing and networked systems in the scope of smart cities, with a major focus on vehicular mobility modeling and vehicle-based sensing. My advisor is Professor Desheng Zhang.
